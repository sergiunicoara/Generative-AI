ATA 27 — Flight Controls — Fly-By-Wire System — Software Update

APPLICABILITY:
Airbus A320-200N series aeroplanes, all manufacturer serial numbers (MSN), equipped
with Flight Management and Guidance Computer (FMGC) standard P/12C or earlier.
Airbus A321-271N series aeroplanes, MSN 7500 through 10420 inclusive, equipped with
FMGC standard P/12C or earlier.

REASON:
Airbus has released a new Flight Management and Guidance Computer (FMGC) software
standard P/12D following identification of a potential incorrect vertical navigation
(VNAV) path calculation under specific atmospheric conditions (ISA+20°C or above,
cruise altitude above FL370, non-standard wind shear profile). The incorrect VNAV
calculation could lead to an uncommanded altitude deviation of up to 200 feet during
cruise phase. This condition, if not corrected, could reduce vertical separation from
other traffic.

This AD supersedes EASA AD 2022-0201 and requires installation of FMGC software
standard P/12D or later, which corrects the vertical navigation algorithm and improves
wind shear compensation.

REQUIRED ACTIONS AND COMPLIANCE TIMES:
(1) Software Update: Within 6 months after the effective date of this AD, install FMGC
software standard P/12D or later in accordance with Airbus Service Bulletin A320-22-
1272, dated January 22, 2024.

(2) Operational Restriction (interim): Until the software update required by paragraph
(1) is accomplished, prior to each flight at or above FL370 with ISA deviation greater
than +18°C, flight crew must select MANAGED SPEED mode and monitor vertical
profile deviations. If deviation exceeds 150 feet, apply OPEN DES or OPEN CLB as
appropriate.

(3) Training: Before operating modified aeroplanes, ensure all line pilots receive
briefing on FMGC P/12D changes as described in Airbus Flight Operations Telex
(FOT) SE-20.00046, dated February 2024.
