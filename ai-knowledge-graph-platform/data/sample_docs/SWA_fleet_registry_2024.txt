OVERVIEW

Southwest Airlines Co. is an American low-cost carrier headquartered in Dallas, Texas.
Southwest operates exclusively Boeing 737 family aircraft and is the world's largest
operator of the Boeing 737. As of January 2024, Southwest operates a fleet that
includes Boeing 737-8 (737 MAX 8) and is transitioning from the 737-700 and 737-800.

IATA Code: WN
ICAO Code: SWA
FAA Certificate: USSWA
Primary Hub: Dallas Love Field (DAL)
Hubs: Dallas Love Field, Chicago Midway, Baltimore/Washington, Denver, Las Vegas

FLEET ACQUISITION HISTORY

Southwest received its first Boeing 737 MAX 8 on August 29, 2017 (N8700L).
All 737 MAX 8 aircraft were grounded on March 13, 2019 per FAA Emergency Order.
Southwest returned 737 MAX 8 to revenue service on March 11, 2021.

Southwest Airlines operates Boeing 737 MAX 8 primarily on medium-haul routes
(1.5 to 4 hours). The aircraft is configured in an all-economy 175-seat layout.
